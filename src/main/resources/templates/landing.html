Where does "Ada" comes from?

We decided to name our website after Ada Lovelace, a famous mathematician who is known as the first computer programmer after realizing that a computer could follow a series of simple instructions to perform a complex task. Her efforts inspire us because she was a woman who balanced her profession and her motherhood.

A woman who loved coding!

Lovelace wrote of how the machine could be programmed with a code to calculate Bernoulli numbers, which some consider to be the first algorithm to be carried out by a machine and thus the first computer program.

To all the women who want to start their tech career...

You can do it too! This website is designed to help you start your tech career with the right foot by providing free learning resources and a job-matching tool you can use to apply to your next tech job!
